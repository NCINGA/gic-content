# Government Type

## Q1: What type of government is it?

1. President (Head of State + strong executive role)
The President is one of the most powerful figures in Sri Lanka
Responsible for national security, big policy decisions, and appointing key officials
2. Prime Minister
Helps run the government day-to-day
Leads the cabinet and supports law-making and administration
3. Parliament (Legislative branch)
Known as the Parliament of Sri Lanka
Members are elected by the people
Their job is to make laws, debate policies, and approve budgets
