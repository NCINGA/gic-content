1.0 Executive Summary 
During the reporting period, the QA team continued supporting multiple projects including ICTA, CEFT, Trendix, 
Xuma, and ISVA External. Major activities focused on test case preparation, user story reviews, API testing, 
deployment support, bug verification, UAT activities, and project documentation reviews. 
Several projects progressed from requirement analysis and test case preparation into active testing and 
deployment stages. Some challenges remained around environment access, Citrix access, pending 
requirement sign-offs, and external dependencies.
