

**Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Foreign Employment & Tourism **
 
The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Foreign Employment & Tourism Hon. Vijitha Herath 
assumed duties at the Ministry on 18 November 2024. 
 
Vision 
To be a responsible nation within the international community and to maintain friendly 
relations with all countries. 
Mission 
The promotion, projection, and protection of Sri Lanka's national interests 
internationally, in accordance with the foreign policy of the Government and to advise 
the Government on managing foreign relations in keeping with Sri Lanka's national 
interests.
