Intern – Quality Assurance 
Reporting Line: Lead – Quality Assurance​
Location: HQ​
Type: Full-Time 
Role Summary: 
As a Software Quality Assurance Intern, you will support the QA team in ensuring 
the delivery of high-quality software products. This role offers hands-on exposure 
to software testing processes, tools, and methodologies while allowing you to 
contribute to real-world projects in a collaborative and fast-paced environment. 
It is an excellent opportunity for someone looking to build a strong foundation in 
software quality assurance and testing within the technology industry. 
Key Responsibilities: 
●​ Test Case Development & Execution: Assist in writing, reviewing, and 
executing test cases based on functional requirements. 
●​ Manual and Performance Testing: Perform manual and performance 
testing of web and mobile applications, as well as APIs, to identify defects 
and usability issues. 
●​ Defect Tracking: Log and document defects clearly using tools such as JIRA 
or similar bug-tracking platforms. 
●​ Collaboration: Work closely with developers and QA team members 
throughout the testing and bug-fixing lifecycle. 
●​ Testing Support: Support regression, smoke, and exploratory testing 
activities to maintain product stability and performance. 
●​ Documentation & Reporting: Document test results and assist in preparing 
QA reports to communicate testing outcomes effectively.

KPIs: 
●​ Test Execution Efficiency: Complete assigned test cases within agreed 
timelines while maintaining accuracy. 
●​ Defect Documentation Quality: Ensure defects are logged with clear,

●​ Basic understanding of software development and testing concepts. 
●​ Familiarity with bug tracking and test management tools (e.g., JIRA, 
TestLink, Excel). 
●​ Detail-oriented with strong analytical and communication skills. 
●​ Willingness to learn, take initiative, and work effectively in a team-oriented 
environment.

Python.

✔​ Hands-on experience in real-world QA workflows.
