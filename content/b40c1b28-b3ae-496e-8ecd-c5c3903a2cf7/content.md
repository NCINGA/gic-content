# Government Service: Driving License Renewal

## Overview
Citizens can renew their driving license online through the Department of Motor Traffic portal.

## Requirements
- Valid NIC (National Identity Card)
- Current driving license (within 30 days of expiry)
- Medical certificate from a registered practitioner

## Process
1. Visit the DMT portal
2. Fill in the renewal application form
3. Upload required documents
4. Make the payment online
5. Visit the nearest DMT office for biometric verification
